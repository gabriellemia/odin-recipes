Petrifying Pumpkin Loaf (vegan)

Sweet tooth playing havoc this halloween? Need a healthier alternative to the treats in the haul? Try this moist, aromatic, warm zezty pumpkin loaf to sate your apetite. Cosy up and enjoy it with a nice warm cup of our Pumpkin nog!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Coconut Flour
  • 1 1/2 Tablespoons Pumpkin Spice Blend
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Can Pumpkin
  • 4 Flax Eggs
  • 3 Tablespoons Agave
  • 1 Tablespoon Coconut Oil, melted
  • 3/4 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 375ÂºF and line a loaf tin with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine coconut flour, pumpkin spice blend, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together canned pumpkin, flax eggs, agave, coconut oil, and vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together canned pumpkin, flax eggs, agave, coconut oil, and vanilla extract.
  5. Fold wet ingredients into dry ingredients, then pour batter into the loaf tin.
  6. Bake until inserted toothpick comes out dry, 45-50 minutes.
  7. Let cool for 20 minutes before slicing.
